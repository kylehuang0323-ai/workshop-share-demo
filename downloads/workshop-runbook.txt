RE&F AI Workshop Runbook (90 minutes)

0-10 min | Opening
- Explain goal: not "watching a demo", but "finishing tasks together".
- Share expected outcomes: speed, quality, confidence.

10-30 min | Demo 1: Information Structuring
- Input: scattered notes / updates
- Output: structured summary with clear headings and actions
- Group practice: participants redo with their own sample

30-55 min | Demo 2: Content Generation and Rewrite
- Input: rough message / report draft
- Output: audience-specific email / briefing note
- Group practice: iterate prompt with role + tone + constraints

55-80 min | Demo 3: Workflow Reuse
- Combine outputs from demo 1 and 2
- Turn repeated steps into a reusable checklist/template
- Group practice: each participant writes one personal workflow

80-90 min | Wrap-up
- Ask each participant to commit one real task for next week
- Share where to access this page and download package
